## Full description
VerifAI detects AI-generated videos directly in your browser — as you scroll through TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, Twitter/X, Reddit, and Facebook.

**How it works:**
• Automatically analyzes videos as you scroll — no tapping or clicking needed
• Right-click any video link → "Check if AI-generated" for instant results
• Uses metadata forensics + Gemini Vision AI — works even when platforms strip metadata
• Shows a red "AI" badge with confidence % directly on the video
• Sends a desktop notification with the result

**Detects videos from:**
Sora, Kling, Runway, Pika, Luma, Hailuo, HeyGen, Synthesia, and 25+ other AI tools

**Three verdict types:**
🤖 AI Generated — created entirely by an AI tool
✏️ AI Edited — real footage with AI effects (face swap, AI audio)
✅ Authentic — real camera footage

**Privacy first:**
No video data is stored. Detection runs via URL analysis — no frame uploads from your browser.

**Supported platforms:**
TikTok · Instagram · YouTube · Twitter/X · Reddit · Facebook · Telegram
